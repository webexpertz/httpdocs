Welcome

The Royal Meteorological Society is the Learned and Professional Society for weather and climate.

Our mission is to advance the understanding of weather and climate, the science and its applications for the benefit of all.

Find out more

Join us

The Society is open to anyone whose profession or interestes are connected with weather and climate.
